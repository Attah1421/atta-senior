Objectives

By the end of the lesson, students will be able to:

  • Define Leadership
  • State three ways by which individuals can become a leader
  • Mention two types of leadership style
  • Discuss three characteristics of effective leadership

Activities

Introduction: Through Q&A, teacher guides learners to brainstorm the purposes of election.

  1. Discuss the meaning of leadership.
  2. Brainstorm ways by which an individual can become a leader.
  3. Write ceiling points on the board.
  4. Discuss characteristics of effective leadership.

Core Points

Leadership: The ability to guide and influence people towards a goal.

Types of Leadership:

  • Autocratic
  • Democratic
  • Laissez-faire
  • Paternalistic
